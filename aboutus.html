Who We Are

A vibrant couple who love family games and share a vision for publishing children's ministry book series. With backgrounds in theology and environmental science, we are committed to serving children from all walks of life. Escape Missions™ is dedicated to publishing faith-based ebooks and books for the kingdom of God. Our mission is to introduce Jesus Christ to children around the world and share His love in all nations.
